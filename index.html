SCUBA DIVING SIMULATION

YOU CAN CONTROL THE GAME MOVEMENTS BY THE FOLLOWING BUTTONS:

W - To move UP .

S - To move DOWN .

ARROW UP - To move ahead .

ARROW DOWN - To move backword .

ARROW RIGHT - To move RIGHT .

ARROW LEFT - To move LEFT .

THIS GAME HAVE VERY SIMPLE RULES TO PLAY YOU JUST HAVE TO MOVE YOUR DIVER AROUND THE MAP.
